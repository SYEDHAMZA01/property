Big Bear Cool Cabins invites you to be our guest in the breathtaking mountains of Southern California. Big Bear Lake is a year-round resort with a beautiful lake for boating and fishing in the summer and the best slopes in Southern California for skiing and snowboarding in the winter. Come escape to Big Bear with its mild summer temperatures and snowy winters. The San Bernardino National Forest is a beautiful area for hiking, biking, and horseback riding. We have a quaint Village full of shops and restaurants, an Big Bear Alpine Zoo, Bear Mountain Golf Course, and much, much more.

Just a two hour drive from Los Angeles or Orange County and three hours from San Diego or Las Vegas, Big Bear Lake is the perfect vacation destination. Rent a cabin on the lake, near the slopes, or next to the hiking trails. Big Bear Cool Cabins has the perfect vacation rental for you. We even have pet friendly cabins available for your four-legged family members as well. We look forward to hosting you soon!
