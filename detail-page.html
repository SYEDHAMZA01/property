WALK TO SLOPES! Comfy Cubby is perfect for everyone. Located in a beautiful, desirable area of Big Bear close to Bear Mountain Ski Resort and just a short drive to the lake, Village, and more. This gorgeous home comes equipped with everything you need for a relaxing mountain getaway!

LIVING SPACE - 2 bedroom, 1.5 bath. Cozy 850+ sq ft home with living room, dining table, a beautiful gas fireplace, and plenty of comfortable living room seating. First bedroom has a queen bed. Second bedroom has a double bed. Extra blankets and pillows are available. 

KITCHEN - Stove, toaster oven, refrigerator, microwave, blender, coffee maker, crockpot, rice cooker, dishes, pots/pans, spice rack, and more. 

AMENITIES - Board games include Cards Against Humanity Family Edition, Chutes and Ladders, and more. This home offers cable TV along with fast WiFi and Blu-ray DVD player. Relax near the cozy fireplace. 

OUTDOORS - Charcoal barbecue, outdoor table, and chairs. Enjoy the fenced yard and large front and side decks! 

Parking - 2 vehicle driveway. 

Other things to note:
Minimum renter age: 21
Maximum occupancy 4
Sorry, the Comfy Cubby does not accept pets
